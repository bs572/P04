Future Into the unknown

Honda is still officially not a part of F1. That being said, until at least 2026, the company still provides Red Bull racing with their powertrains, built and maintained in Sakura Japan. Red Bull and Alpha Tauri compete under the Red Bull Powertrains name, but the power unit is still a totally Honda component. The 2026 regulations will change the power units significantly, removing the MGU-H, but keeping MGU-K. It is rumoured that Honda will make a complete return to Formula 1 in 2026, by buying out the Alpha Tauri team from Red Bull, as the main team is unwilling to part with any large portion of itself.
